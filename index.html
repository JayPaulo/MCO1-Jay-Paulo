Authobiography

My name is Jay B. Paulo, I was born on June 22, 2004. I have a 2 sisters and 2 brothers, My parents are Nenita Paulo and Gabriel Paulo. One of my sister is Graduate as Civil Engineer and my other siblings they didn’t finish college. My mother is a house wife while my father is a farmer.

When I was a child, I really want to become adult because it seems like you’re happier when you’re older, but that’s what I don’t know. As you get older, you have more and more responsibilities in life, and life sinks deeper into sadness. When I was young, I should have enjoyed my childhood, not wanted to grow old although aging cannot be stopped. When I was child I always play with my friends even though I get scolded when I get home and also we going to the river to swim/bath. When I was high school I didn’t take my classes seriously and I was just chill and I met a lot friends but I really had one friend she was like sister to me and we went everywhere together but she transferred to another school but we were in grade 10 that time. When I was senior high school I took a strand called ABM and I passed it and now in college when you think what you did before, it’s like you’re laughing at it, but it’s like it’s fun to be a kid, not like now when you have so many thoughts like how you’re going to survive in college because they give so much activities.

To the people who push me to who I am today are my friends, family, and to the people who didn’t believe in me. They are the reason why I am working hard to finish my studies so that I can repay what they did for me and the people who don’t believe in me, so that I can show them that I can do it.
